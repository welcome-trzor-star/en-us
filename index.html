Ultimate Security

Trezór hardware wallets represent the gold standard in crypto security. By keeping your private keys offline in a secure chip, your digital assets remain protected from online threats, malware, and potential breaches. Each transaction requires physical verification, ensuring that only you have control over your funds.

Easy to Use

Despite its sophisticated security features, Trezór offers an intuitive interface that makes managing cryptocurrencies accessible to everyone. The companion Trezór Suite software provides a seamless experience for sending, receiving, and trading crypto assets, all while maintaining the highest security standards.

Wide Compatibility

Supporting over 1000+ cryptocurrencies, Trezór ensures you can secure virtually any digital asset. From Bitcoin and Ethereum to the latest DeFi tokens, your Trezór device becomes your universal key to the crypto ecosystem. Regular firmware updates add support for new coins and enhanced features.

Backup & Recovery

Never worry about losing access to your funds. Trezór implements industry-standard recovery seeds, allowing you to restore your wallet on any device. The 24-word recovery phrase serves as your ultimate backup, ensuring your assets remain accessible even if your device is lost or damaged.
